Who We Are

A sharp, nimble consulting partner built for modern India

AIMM Consulting LLP is a newly incorporated, Gurgaon-based boutique management consultancy. We operate out of Greenwood City, Sector 45 — one of the city's key strategic residential and business zones.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers to common questions about our consultancy structure.

We work primarily with growing startups, SMEs, and mid-sized corporate firms in Gurgaon and the wider Delhi NCR area. We customize our advisory frameworks to match the constraints and growth trajectories typical of these organization sizes.

Our project engagements generally run between 4 to 12 weeks depending on scope complexity. For ongoing operational support and advisory, we operate on flexible monthly retainer agreements with a 30-day notice cycle.

We adopt a hybrid structure. Discovery sessions and strategic reviews are frequently executed in person at our client offices in Gurgaon/NCR. Execution support, analytics, and drafting are handled by our consultants from our office in Greenwood City, Sector 45.

We believe in transparent, value-aligned pricing. Depending on your choice of engagement model, we offer fixed-scope pricing for projects and predictable monthly rates for retainers or fractional placements. We don't charge hidden hourly rates.
