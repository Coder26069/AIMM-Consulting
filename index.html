Who We Are

A specialist data consultancy, built for the AI era

AIMM Consulting LLP is a Gurgaon-based boutique consultancy focused entirely on enterprise data: Master Data Management, Data Quality, Data Governance, and Product Information Management. We don't spread thin across generic strategy work — this is the one thing we go deep on.

Founder Manish Goel brings 15+ years as an Enterprise Solution Architect across Informatica MDM/IDMC, Salsify ProductXM, Syndigo CXH, Oracle PDH/EDQ, Reltio MDM, Stibo STEP, Informatica Product 360 and Akeneo — platform-agnostic expertise applied to your specific stack, not a single vendor's playbook. Programs are delivered in agile, business-aligned sprints under Ankita Pandey's Scrum leadership, not multi-year waterfall rollouts.

Master Data Management (MDM) creates one trusted, consistent record for core entities like customers, products, or vendors across all your systems. Without it, AI, analytics, and reporting initiatives end up built on duplicate, conflicting, or stale data.
